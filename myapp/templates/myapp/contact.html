Bro Mathin Attah

Coordinator
PROFILE

Bro Martin Atah was born in the family of Mr and MrsJohn nweke Atah of Umuaru clan in Umuzocha Village Awka, in Awka-South, L.G.A, Anambra State. Martin Speaks,Igbo, English and Yoruba fluently. He Joined Catholic Charistmatics Renewal in the year 2000 /2001, and has been in leadership since 2008. Bro Martin become the General Coordinator of Saviour's Will Prayer Group in the year 2019 after general election. Martin was also elected as the Assistant Diocesan Chairman, CCRN, Awka Diocese in the same year 2019 due to his transparency and life of emulation and discipline. Bro Martin like St. Stephen is filled with Spiritual gifts that made him outstanding among equals. In academic-wise, Bro posess the following qualifications:- B.eng, Pgd, M.eng, Professional qualifications- COREN, NSE
